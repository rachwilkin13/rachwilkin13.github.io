Nestled in the heart of the Essex countryside, our campsite offers an idyllic escape for nature lovers, adventure seekers, and those looking to unwind in a serene environment. Our site features spacious grass pitches, and a variety of outdoor activities to make your stay memorable.

What We Offer:

  • Pitches: Spacious and well-maintained pitches for tents, caravans, motorhomes and rooftents

  • Activities: Enjoy canoeing, paddleboarding locally creating unforgettable memories with family and friends. We offer canoe hire for a delightful paddle to the local pub and paddleboard hire for those looking to explore the river in style

  • Walks: Discover the beauty of the Salt Marsh Coastline with its picturesque landscapes and serene walking trails

  • Environment: We welcome your furry friends and provide information on local dog-friendly pubs like The Muddy Duck

  • Convenient Facilities: Clean toilets and hot showers to ensure a comfortable stay.
